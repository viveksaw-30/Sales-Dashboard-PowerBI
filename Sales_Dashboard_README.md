# 📊 Business Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing business sales performance across countries, product categories, and time periods. Provides real-time KPIs and drill-down insights for data-driven decision making.

## Dashboard Features
- **Sales by Period by Year** — Line chart tracking sales trends (Daily / Monthly / Weekly)
- **Region-wise Performance** — Horizontal bar chart comparing sales across UK, Germany, France, EIRE, Netherlands, Australia
- **Category-wise Sales** — Bar chart breaking down sales by product name/category
- **Top 5 Products** — Ranked bar chart of best-selling products (Zinc T-Light, Zinc Willie Winkie, etc.)
- **Top 5 Customers** — Bar chart identifying highest-value customers by total quantity
- **Dynamic Filters** — Slicers for Country (Australia, Austria, Bahrain...) and Period (Daily, Monthly, Weekly)
